Croissant

Ingredient

  • 500 g flour
  • 25 g baker's yeast
  • 25 cl of milkr
  • 15 g of salt
  • 1 glass of hot water
  • 50 g sugar
  • 200 g semi-soft butter (must have the same consistency as the dough)

Preparation steps

  1. Mix the yeast with hot water. Pour over 1/4 of the flour. Beat well, let rise for 15 minutes.
  2. Add the rest of the flour, sugar, salt and hot milk. Mix well. The dough should be elastic and no longer stick. Finish the kneading on the table.
  3. Roll out into a square. Add the butter in the center and close the 4 sides of the dough (like an envelope on the butter). Roll out lengthwise. (give a ride).
  4. Fold in three. Leave to rest for 15 minutes.
  5. Make the croissants. Cut the dough into 10cm x 15cm x 15cm triangles. Moisten lightly with cold water. Roll starting from the base. For my part, I use a system that I learned at the Hotel School to know how many turns I have given: I press / mark the dough with one finger (1 turn), 2 fingers (2 turns), etc. . I give in all 4 to 5 turns.
  6. Make 10cm x 15cm x 15cm triangles. Lightly moisten with cold water, then brown with egg.
  7. Let rise for an hour. Bake at 200-210 Â° C for 10 to 15 minutes until golden brown.
  8. Enjoy!
