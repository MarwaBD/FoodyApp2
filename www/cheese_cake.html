Cheese cake

Ingredient

  • 330 g of Philadephia cheese
  • 250 g of speculoos
  • 110 g sugar
  • the juice of 4 lemons
  • raspberry coulis (to serve)
  • 330 g cheese
  • 120g fresh raspberries, plus a few for garnish
  • 90 g melted butter
  • 1 vanilla pod

Preparation steps

  1. Preheat your oven to (160 ° C)
  2. Place the speculoos in the bowl of the Thermomix and reduce them to a powder by mixing them for 10 sec, speed 5. Pour the melted butter into the bowl and mix for 10 sec, speed 5. Place in the bottom of a buttered springform pan. Reserve in the refrigerator.
  3. Prepare the cheesecake by mixing the cheese and sugar for 30 sec, speed 5.
  4. Insert the whisk into the food processor, add the eggs then beat 40 sec, speed 4
  5. Add the lemon juice and mix again for 40 sec, speed 4.
  6. Add the raspberries to the bowl of the Thermomix. Stir with the spatula to incorporate them without crushing them
  7. Pour the preparation into the springform pan. Bake for 50 min of cooking on th. 5-6 (160 ° C). Let cool completely in the refrigerator before eating
  8. Enjoy!
