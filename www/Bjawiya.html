Bjawiya

Ingredient

  • 150 grams of blanched almonds
  • 50 grams of hazelnuts
  • 50 grams of walnut kernels
  • 25 grams of pine nuts
  • 20 grams of pistachios
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • 150 grams of caster sugar
  • Half a lemon
  • 1 sachet of vanilla sugar
  • Powdered pistachios and pine nuts for garnish

Preparation steps

  1. Prepare the dried fruits separately by placing in the oven at 140 Â° C, the hazelnuts for about 20 min, the almonds for 15 min, then the walnuts for 5 min. Remove the skin from the roasted hazelnuts. Coarsely crush the almonds, walnuts, hazelnuts, pistachios and pine nuts.
  2. Squeeze half a lemon and reserve it for the syrup.
  3. To make the syrup, cook the sugar with 10 cl of water. When boiling, add the lemon juice. Check the doneness by plunging the blade of a knife into cold water, remove a little sugar, put the blade back into the water.
  4. Then slide your finger over the sugar to obtain a pearl.
  5. Syrup is poured over the crushed dried fruits and mixed with a wooden spatula.
  6. Lightly oil the work surface and the rolling pin, place the Bjaouia and flatten it. Repeat the operation on a plate.
  7. On the baking sheet, cut large strips of Bjawia and shape them into a square.
  8. For the decoration, delicately immerse the surface of the latter in the pistachio powder.
  9. At the end, add a pinion on top then arrange the pieces of Bjawia in a dish
  10. Enjoy!
